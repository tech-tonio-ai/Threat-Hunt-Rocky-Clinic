//Rather than wiping everything, the operator removes only certain traces, signalling deliberate cleanup. The selective deletions targeted multiple evidence categories across two system log files.

//Scope:

//host: rocky83.zi5bvzlx0idetcyt0okhu05hda.cx.internal.cloudapp.net
//logs in scope: /var/log/secure AND /var/log/messages
//time: between 2026-02-11 16:13 and 16:16 UTC
//How many distinct sed -i delete operations did the operator run across both log files combined?

//Format: integer

DeviceProcessEvents
| where TimeGenerated between (datetime(2026-02-04) .. datetime(2026-02-15))
| where DeviceName has "rocky83"
| where ProcessCommandLine has_any ("/var/log/secure","/var/log/messages") and ProcessCommandLine contains "sed -i"
| where ProcessCommandLine !contains "sudo"
| project TimeGenerated, ProcessCommandLine, InitiatingProcessCommandLine
| sort by TimeGenerated asc
| distinct ProcessCommandLine
